FOOD RECEIPE-BIRYANI

Chicken biryani is a flavorful dish combining marinated chicken with aromatic rice, cooked to perfection and cherished for its rich taste and cultural significance.

INGREDIENTS:
  • 2 cups basmati rice
  • 500g chicken, cut into pieces
  • 2 onions, thinly sliced
  • 2 tomatoes, chopped
  • 1/2 cup yogurt
  • 2 tablespoons ginger-garlic paste
  • 2 green chilies, slit
  • 1/4 cup chopped mint leaves
  • 1/2 teaspoon turmeric powder
  • 1 teaspoon red chili powder
  • 1/4 cup chopped coriander leaves
  • 1 teaspoon biryani masala powder
  • 4 cups water
  • 4 tablespoons ghee or oil
  • Salt to taste
WHOLE SPICES:
  • 2 bay leaves
  • 4 cloves
  • 4 green cardamom pods
  • 2-inch cinnamon stick
  • 1 star anise
  • 1 teaspoon cumin seeds
PROCEDURE:
  1. Rinse the basmati rice under cold water until the water runs clear. Soak the rice in water for 30 minutes, then drain.
  2. Marinate the chicken with yogurt, ginger-garlic paste, red chili powder, turmeric powder, and salt. Let it marinate for at least 30 minutes.
  3. In a large pan or pot, heat ghee or oil over medium heat. Add the whole spices and saut√© until fragrant.
  4. Add the sliced onions and green chilies. Cook until the onions turn golden brown.
  5. Add the marinated chicken pieces and cook until they are partially cooked, about 5-7 minutes.
  6. Add the chopped tomatoes, mint leaves, and coriander leaves. Cook for another 5 minutes.
  7. Add the soaked and drained rice to the pot. Gently mix everything together.
  8. Pour in the water and season with salt. Bring the mixture to a boil.
  9. Once it starts boiling, reduce the heat to low, cover the pot with a tight-fitting lid, and let it cook for about 15-20 minutes, or until the rice is fully cooked and the chicken is tender.
  10. Once done, remove from heat and let it sit, covered, for another 10 minutes.
  11. Gently fluff the biryani with a fork and serve hot with raita or salad.
